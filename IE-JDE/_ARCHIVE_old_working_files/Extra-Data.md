**Notes and methodology**

1. **Currency conversion:** GoTo figures were originally reported in Indonesian rupiah (IDR) billions. They were converted to USD millions by multiplying by 1,000 (to turn IDR billion into IDR million) and dividing by the average annual exchange rate provided by the user (14 550 IDR/USD for 2020, 14 300 for 2021, 14 850 for 2022 and 15 250 for 2023).
2. **Invisible labor wedge:** Calculated as `GMV – Net Revenue`. It approximates the value of transactions not captured as corporate revenue (e.g., payments to drivers, merchants and sellers).
3. **Indonesia isolation:** Grab and Sea do not disclose revenue or GMV by country in their audited filings. Following the user’s instruction, 50 % of total ASEAN GMV, revenue and incentives were allocated to Indonesia. These entries are flagged as estimates.
4. **GoTo pro‑forma data:** GoTo did not exist as a consolidated entity before 2021. Historical (2020‑2021) figures are derived from the company’s pro‑forma financial statements prepared in connection with the IPO. These figures combine Gojek and Tokopedia as if the entities were consolidated from January 1, 2020 and therefore may not be directly comparable to later consolidated results[assets.tokopedia.net](https://assets.tokopedia.net/asts/goto/GoTo%20FY21%20Earnings%20Presentation.pdf#:~:text=Group%20GTV%20461%2C602%20330%2C176%20139%2C998,1%2C497%201%2C418%20Cost%20of%20Revenue).
